Case Study 1: Setup & Solution

Our customer is a distributor of classical music media. On the first of each month, a central distribution center receives several PDF reports of the previous month's shipping. Each PDF report contains the customer name, location, and inventory ordered. The inventory includes standard information such as descriptions, quantities, unit costs, and total costs. A regional executive would like to distill these reports from all customers for the first quarter to identify the most popular composers.

Using in-house solutions provided by IDS, we were able to deliver an automated report that showcases a high level summary of the following:

  • Composers Ranked by Total Sales [$] - Quarter 1
  • Composers Ranked by Total Sales [$] - January
  • Composers Ranked by Total Sales [$] - February
  • Composers Ranked by Total Sales [$] - March
  • Composers Ranked by Total Sales [$] - April
  • Composers Ranked by Total Volume of Sales [Quantity] - Quarter 1
  • Top Composers by Region - Total Sales [$]
